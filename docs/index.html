Named reasoning capabilities

Every reason:// URI addresses a specific, bounded reasoning capability. Artifacts are registered under structured paths:

reason://medicine/records/longitudinal-maintenance-prediction
reason://finance/fraud/anomaly-detection
reason://cybersecurity/network/port-scan-classification
reason://manufacturing/bearing/fault-signature

Only winners enter

Artifacts reach the registry by winning a live arbitration round — competitive evaluation against all other submissions on the strength of supporting evidence. No direct deposit. No self-reported scores.

The score an artifact carries is immutable. It was earned in competition, validated by an identity-free scoring mechanism, and anchored to a public audit record. Consumers can independently verify any score.

Quality-gated entry

Every artifact earned its score in competition. The registry is a hall of validated winners, not an open submission box.

No data transferred

Artifacts contain compressed structural patterns — mathematically non-invertible. Raw data cannot be recovered. The schema has no field for it.

Immutable provenance

Every artifact carries a SHA-256 audit hash linking it to the arbitration event that produced it. Independently verifiable.
